A material for thin film magnetic head slider particularly suited for high recording, which is obtained by sintering a mixture consisting essentially of:5 to 45% by weight of titanium carbide component;0.2 to 10% by weight of zirconium oxide;0.05 to 2% by weight of oxide of yttrium;0.2 to 25% by weight of aluminum nitride; and the balance of Al.sub.2 O.sub.3 component substituted with 0.5 to 8% by weight being at least one machinability agent selected from the group consisting of oxides of Mg, Ca, B, Ni and Cr and complex oxides thereof.
